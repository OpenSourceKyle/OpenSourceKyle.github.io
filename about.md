# Performative Art Project

This is a **performative art project** in the practice of being more open, honest, and vulnerable. With that comes all of my imperfection that I had spent years hiding, fixing, or simply lying about. Why live like that anymore? I am exhausted of being anyone else.

## Is this really performative art project?

Well, yes -- I said it a bunch of times. Though more seriously, I am labelling it as such right now because there is still some underlying hesitation to admit that is just my life -- that fear, shame, or guilt coming from somewhere. But I know little by little, I can better accept all these parts of me.
