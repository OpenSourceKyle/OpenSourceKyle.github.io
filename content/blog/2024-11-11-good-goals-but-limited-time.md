I took a while to refine some goals and used good ole ChatGPT to help critique them better. My only concern is for the remainder of the year; I plan to be traveling a lot (more into the beginning of the year). That said, none of this is impossible, but my short-term travel is a higher priority (more of a limited chance for some of these trips versus my other longer-term goals that will always be here).

My best way to achieve these is to religiously use my Google Calendar (which is connected to some of my biz automations and calendar as well) to plan time, achieve, make money, but most importantly -- learn and enjoy my life. I'm testing some timers (Toggl) to better see where time is spent and measure my effort, but it's a bit of a learning curve to incorporate all this, so I'll keep persevering and make it stick.

On the biz side, I'm already making money, but longer-term I think I am too broad and need to actually have a niche. Luckily, I've already determined all that, but I won't have time this year to build that part. Maybe the niche will be next year's biz. Living long-term in Mexico and figuring out life has been a steeper learning curve, but I don't regret it at all -- life for me has only improved and will continue to do so. In fact, part of my niche revolves around that but for a selected demographic that I fall in (that most people do not).

All that said, life is wonderful. I'm in great shape after another bout of food poisoning. I have a call tomorrow for a potential 3rd client, and I'm traveling with a best friend over Mexico, Chile, and maybe Brazil to finish out the year (and tentatively Colombia in the spring) in the next week.

# GOALS (pasted)

## BIZ Goals​

- Income Goal: Earn $1k+ monthly with 5+ clients.
- Professional Online Ecosystem: Establish a professional online presence, including a finalized website, completed articles, and a few key videos that effectively introduce you to potential clients.
- Work-Life Balance: Work only 15-20 hours per week (3-4 hours per day).


### Subgoals

- Website Redesign: Complete the redesign of your website by 15 Dec 2024 to create a more professional and user-friendly experience.
- Fix Articles: Update and fix articles with broken images to ensure all content is polished.
- Create Key Content: Focus on creating essential content (articles, blogs, and videos) that represent your services clearly.
- Work Allocation:
    - Standard Work (SW): Minimize to 25% of your work time (coaching to maintain current income).
    - Improvement Work (IW): Maximize to 75% of your work time (focus on acquiring more clients to grow income).

## FITNESS Goals​

- Running Goal: Be able to run a half-marathon (21km) in under 2 hours by February 2025 and choose a run competition to participate in.
- Physique Goal: Increase muscle size in key areas (arms, chest, legs) while reducing waist circumference, measured with a tape measure, to achieve a balanced and lean appearance.


### Subgoals

- Weekly Measurement: Measure waist circumference, arm, chest, and leg size weekly to track muscle gain and weight loss progress.
- Running Routine: Run 3 times per week (approximately 4 hours total).
- Gym Routine: Go to the gym 4 times per week (approximately 8 hours total).
- Nutrition: Maintain a consistent eating schedule to support fitness goals.

## SOCIAL Goals​

- Social Confidence: Become a very social person who can talk with anyone despite feeling fear, without letting it stop you.
- Network Growth: Grow your local network with intelligent, professional people (ideally a mix of foreigners and Mexicans).


### Subgoals

- Weekly Outings: Commit to 2 weekly outings to explore new areas, meet new people, or gain new experiences (including transport: approximately 4-8 hours per week).
