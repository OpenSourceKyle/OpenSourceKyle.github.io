We think it matters what we say, but it doesn't

- 100% agree. What i've found actually matters is INTENTION. As in, going in with a positive intention 
  - "I wanna have a good conversation with this girl"
  - "I wanna get to know her and see if we vibe!"
  - "I'll give myself permission to suck, and it'll be ok if I'm not perfect. Everything will be ok."

You can think of "intention" as "philosophy" as well. Going in with a positive PHILOSOPHY or INTENTION is all that really matters.

Another really good intention/philosophy that helps is "I have no expectations; only curiousity."

Or "I'll just see what happens."

Or "No matter what happens, everything will be ok."

## Process

### Warmup: INTERNAL

- Listen to music
- Eye contact + smile

### Warmup: EXTERNAL

- Greet (Hola, Buen día, Que onda)
- View everyone as current friends or lovers
- Give the vibes of "love" to everyone

### Direct

- Ask questions
- Compliment on non-beauty characteristic
- Make positive assumptions
- I'm new here... what's something I should see

### Approach

- I thought you were cute and wanted to say hello
- Doing some errands
- Well hey I don't have much time, but let's get a coffee sometime on a hot date
