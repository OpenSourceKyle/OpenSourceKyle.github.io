These posts are very much like a journal or diary -- they are not meant to be perfect nor reflect my beliefs well. I use these to get thoughts out of my head -- some turn into ideas and others are best forgotten (or left here).

Most, although not all of the time, I tend to write from low moods. Some of these writings might be old and come from different sources; they might have been edited to make better sense in this current format while retaining the gist from the original. I also try to edit them to remove any personally identifiable information while communicating in an neutral and ambiguous way, which may seem clunky at times.

**Regardless, here they are -- without guarantee or expectation. I ask that you read them with kindness and understanding**
