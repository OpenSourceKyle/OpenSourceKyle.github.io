Howdy peeps. I had a good weekend. A private goal has been to get out more little by little (which I already do a lot) but to specifically see more cultural events and museums (Mexico City is the 3rd in the world I think for museums)? This is a great motivator to get my sleep/diet/etc. in line because when it's not... it sucks. 

Without giving it much pressure, I've been toying with the idea of another half marathon [~21km] this year (or beginning of next year)... but before that, I want to keep working out my logistics of running (diet, routes, schedule, etc.). I'm still very low mileage to make too big of plans, but I'm happy. This week, I have some admin stuff to work on.---
