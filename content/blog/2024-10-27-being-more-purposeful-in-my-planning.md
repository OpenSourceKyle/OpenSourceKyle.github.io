This will be my 3rd trial running Modafinil. Yes, it's a fad nootropic, but it is used on-label for daytime tiredness and sleep issues. Although I'm optimizing my sleep as much as possible before my sleep study (for a CPAP) in Jan, Moda has seemed to help me with the days that I have incredible tiredness (i.e. literally can't think or function). I am super sensitive to it, so I have it dosed very low and spaced to not give me insomnia. Overall, while not a silver bullet, it seems to work very well and especially helps me function in Spanish if I am tired.
